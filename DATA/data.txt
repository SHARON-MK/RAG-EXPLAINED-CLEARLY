1. Company Overview

Mentor Bro is a technology-focused mentorship and career development platform headquartered in Calicut, Kerala, India. The organization is built with a mission to transform aspiring developers into highly skilled, industry-ready professionals through deep conceptual training and practical implementation.

Mentor Bro focuses on real engineering knowledge, architecture-level understanding, and long-term career growth rather than superficial tool-based learning. The platform emphasizes practical exposure, real-world project development, and strong foundational understanding of software systems.
